### Installation

## Quick Setup

1. Open this webpage (via "Desktop Mode" on Steam Deck)
2. Choose a video and click "Copy"
3. Open the Konsole app
4. Paste and hit "Enter"
5. Wait until the download and installation completes
6. Return to "Gaming Mode" > Settings > Customization
7. Choose your Bootup Video

## Manual Setup

1. Choose a video and click "Download"
2. Move the downloaded video to the following directory (create the folders below if they do not exist):

On SteamOS:

```home/deck/.steam/root/config/uioverrides/movies/```

On Windows (from your Steam installation location):

```Steam/config/uioverrides/movies/```

3. Return to "Gaming Mode" or "Big Picture Mode" > Settings > Customization
4. Choose your Bootup Video
