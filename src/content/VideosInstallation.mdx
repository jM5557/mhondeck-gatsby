## Quick Setup (SteamOS)
<br />
- Visit this page via "Desktop Mode"
- Choose a video below and click "Copy"
- Open the Konsole app
- Paste and hit "Enter"
- Wait until the download and installation completes
- Go to "Gaming Mode" > Settings > Customization
- Choose your Bootup Video
</div>
<div>
## Manual Setup (SteamOS / Windows)
<br />
- Choose a video below and click "Download"
- Move the downloaded video to the following directory 

    **Note: Create the folders below if they do not exist**

    <br />
    On SteamOS:

    ```home/deck/.steam/root/config/uioverrides/movies/```
    <br />
    On Windows (from your Steam installation location):

    ```Steam/config/uioverrides/movies/```
    <br />
- Go to "Gaming Mode" (on SteamOS) or "Steam Big Picture Mode"
- Navigate to Settings > Customization
- Choose your Bootup Video
</div>
