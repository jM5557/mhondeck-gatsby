## Add Artwork Manually
### Setup
   - Locate the game in your Steam Library from a Collection (or click "Uncategorized")
   - *Grid* 
        
        Right-click the game and select **Manage** > "Set Custom Artwork"
   - *Hero and Logo*
        
        From the game's page, right click anywhere and choose "Set Custom Background" or "Set Custom Logo"
   - *Icon*
        
        From the game's page (or right-clicking the Grid tile) visit **Settings > Properties**
