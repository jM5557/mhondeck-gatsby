    ### Setup
   
   - Visit "Gaming Mode" (SteamOS) or "Steam Big Picture Mode"
   - Open your Steam Library
   - Select a Game
   - Navigate to `Settings > Change Artwork...` (to view the SteamGridDB dashboard)
   - Navigate to "Manage" (last tab)
   - Click the Folder icon for each artwork type
   - Browse for artwork
